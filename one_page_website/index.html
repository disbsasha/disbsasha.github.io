I dig into my wallet to find only the mere reminisce of what once was currency in the form of loose change that I acquired throughout the semester. Begrudgingly, I collect my loose change and stash it into my pocket and lumber on full well realizing the public humiliation I faced the last time I paid in loose change. Alas I stumble into the humble but great establishment named Joe's Pizza. The jolly thick accented Italian man asks me what I will be having today. "Two cheese," I manage to speak out as I am dreading the next moments to come. He flashes me a smile and plumps the slices into the noble pizza oven. I quickly start giving him a fair warning before he may even speak a few words, "I'm about to pay in quarters please don't judge me I go to college." He shrugs and openly accepts my coins one by one. The man doesn't even look down at the wreckage of shillings slowly collecting at his palms. "How much is that?" he asks. "5 dollars," I replied briskly with chagrin. He shrugs again and clumps my coins into the register. He returns a few moments later with an extra slice and a coke and hands them over to me. At that moment I felt a rush of heavenly aura fall over me. I take a bite. I am euphoric. As I am enjoying my slice the man keeps speaking to me and calling me 'miss.' I turn around to see what he was indicating towards.
